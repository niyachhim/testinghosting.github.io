About Us

Worldwide Freight & Logistics (Pvt) Ltd., is a privately owned Freight Forwarding company in Pakistan, looking after the interests of United American Line (registered in USA) by representing them, as their agent in Pakistan. The company was founded by a group of professionals in the freight industry with a diverse mix of multi-modal expertise, International Relations & an extensive experience of the Local Market requirements. Our team would like to see themselves, as a partner to their clients in resolving their issues with the great technical capabilities at their disposal. The company would also like to see that high service standards are maintained and exceeded. International freight forwarding norms in the fields of conventional imports and exports as well as supply chain, with the new concept of 3PL /4PL and Contract Logistics, are always kept in mind, while handling the shipments.
