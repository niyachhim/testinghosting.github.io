Air Freight

Worldwide Freight & Logistics (Pvt) Ltd. is an international forwarder specialized in air transportation, forwarding your shipments from and to all destinations of the world. Our well trained and experienced team always strives, to find the most cost efficient solutions to forward your freight by air.
We believe in the continuous development of our business and hence keep ourselves updated with the latest development in the freight industry. We also comply with the latest international air cargo security regulations.
By using our international network of agents and relations, we make sure our clients have the best forwarding solutions for their cargo.
Our Air Freight services:
· Import & export
· Service options:- express, economy and deferred
· Domestic and international distribution
· Dangerous Goods Handling
· Customs Clearance through Contracted Agents

Sea Freight

We are the container transportation agent of choice for retailers and manufacturers both, providing cost effective and reliable services to the industry.
Worldwide Freight & Logistics (Pvt) Ltd. is a major operator of worldwide logistics and a recognized force in the container shipping industry. We have very close relations with the key personnel, at all the major container shipping lines, allowing us to provide high quality full container load transportation services with competitive rates. The priority is also given to the urgent Cargos,when it comes to the space confirmations against the requests from the shippers.

Transportation

Trucking We offer (LTL) less than truck load and (FTL) full truck load services all over country. So whether you’re moving one pallet of freight or a full truck we can accommodate your shipping needs. We understand your needs for a cost-effective, secure and reliable network of freight transportation trucking services with the latest technological solutions to arrange domestic and international transportation on the best terms possible.

Supply Chain Management


Supply chain management is a seamless integrated solution to manage a network of businesses for each industry, processes and activities involving procurement of raw materials, manufacturing and distribution management of Finished Goods.
You can trust that WFL has the technology, solutions and expertise to keep your business ahead of the market needs.
